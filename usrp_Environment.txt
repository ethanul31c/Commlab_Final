------CONDA 環境設置-------
1. 確保電腦有conda (Miniconda就可以)
2. 創建python3.9的環境(名稱usrp_env)
    conda create -n usrp_env python=3.9
    conda activate usrp_env
    (不用的時候 conda deactivate)
3. 確保前面有(usrp_env)才算有activate成功

------VSCode Terminal 啟動設定檔-------
1. Set-ExecutionPolicy -Scope CurrentUser -ExecutionPolicy RemoteSigned -Force
2. Get-ExecutionPolicy -Scope CurrentUser
    要顯示RemoteSigned
3. notepad $PROFILE
4. 把以下兩行貼到notepad裡存檔:
    Set-ExecutionPolicy -Scope Process -ExecutionPolicy Bypass
    & "C:\Users\Ethan\miniconda3\shell\condabin\conda-hook.ps1"
    $env:Path += ";C:\Program Files\UHD\bin;C:\Program Files\UHD\lib"
    $env:UHD_PKG_PATH = "C:\Program Files\UHD"
5. 重開VSCode 應該可以正常conda activate
